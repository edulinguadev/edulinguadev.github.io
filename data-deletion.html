Data Deletion Request - Prüfungsweg für Telc C1 HS App

This page provides information on how to request guidance regarding data deletion for the Prüfungsweg für Telc C1 HS application provided by EduLingua.

Our Data Practices

The Prüfungsweg für Telc C1 HS application is ad-supported and uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. EduLingua does not directly collect or store any personally identifiable information from users of the Application. We do not require user registration.

Data collection for advertising purposes is handled by Google AdMob, as detailed in our Privacy Policy.

How to Request Guidance on Data Deletion

While EduLingua does not directly store your personal data, data is collected by our advertising partner, Google AdMob, to provide advertisements. If you wish to understand how to manage your ad preferences (such as opting out of personalized ads by resetting your advertising ID) or request guidance on data deletion related to AdMob services used through our app, please contact us.

Please send an email to:

laxeneo@gmail.com

Subject Line: Data Deletion Request - Prüfungsweg für Telc C1 HS App

Please include this exact subject line so we can best assist you.

Additional Information

You can also refer to our main Privacy Policy for more information on user rights and how data is handled by third-party services.

What to Include in Your Request

  • Clear identification that you are requesting guidance on data deletion
  • The name of the app (Prüfungsweg für Telc C1 HS)
  • Any specific concerns or questions you have about data handling
  • Your preferred method of response

Response Time: We typically respond to data deletion guidance requests within 2-3 business days.
