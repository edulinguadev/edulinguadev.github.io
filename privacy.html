Privacy Policy for EduLingua Apps

Last updated: June 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the "Prüfungsweg für Telc C1 HS" mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application"), developed and provided by EduLingua (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "our").

This Application is offered as an ad-supported service, free of charge. This policy is intended to inform you about how information is handled when you use our Application, with a specific focus on data processing by our third-party advertising partner, Google AdMob.

1. Information We Do Not Directly Collect

We, EduLingua, want to be clear:

  • No Registration Required: You do not need to create an account or register to use our Application.
  • No Direct Collection of Personal Information: We do not directly collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information from you, such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address, through the Application.

2. Information Collected by Our Advertising Partner (Google AdMob)

To support the Application and provide it to you free of charge, we integrate Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google. When you use our Application, AdMob may automatically collect and process certain information to serve you advertisements (which may be personalized or non-personalized).

The types of information AdMob may collect include, but are not limited to:

  • Device Information: Type of mobile device, operating system version, mobile network information.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Your device's unique advertising ID (e.g., Google Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS).
  • IP Address: Which can be used to infer your general geographic location.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about the ads displayed to you and your interaction with them (e.g., clicks, views).
  • Application Usage Data: Information about how you interact with this Application (e.g., features used, session duration), which helps in serving relevant ads.

We, EduLingua, do not have direct access to or control over the specific individual data points collected by AdMob beyond aggregated or anonymized reports that may be available through AdMob's dashboard. AdMob processes this data in accordance with its own privacy policies.

For detailed information on how Google AdMob collects, uses, and protects your information, please review Google's policies:

  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Google's main Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How Information Collected by AdMob is Used

The information collected by Google AdMob through our Application is primarily used for the following purposes:

  • To display advertisements within the Application.
  • To personalize the advertising content you see (if you have consented to personalized ads or not opted out, depending on your region and device settings).
  • To measure the effectiveness and reach of advertising campaigns.
  • To improve AdMob's services, including fraud prevention and ensuring the security of ad serving.

4. Location Information

This Application does not itself actively request or collect precise real-time location information (e.g., GPS data) from your device. However, our advertising partner, Google AdMob, may infer your general location from your IP address to serve more relevant ads or for their analytical purposes, as described in their privacy policies.

5. Sharing of Information

  • EduLingua: We do not directly collect personal information from you, and therefore, we do not share such information with third parties.
  • Google AdMob: Google AdMob operates under its own privacy policy regarding the data it collects and may share it with its partners as described therein. We recommend you review AdMob's privacy policy (linked in Section 2) for details.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) comply with a legal obligation; (b) protect and defend our rights or property; (c) prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Application; (d) protect the personal safety of users of the Application or the public; or (e) protect against legal liability.

6. Your Choices and Rights Regarding Advertising

You have choices regarding the collection and use of your information for personalized advertising:

  • Opting Out of Personalized Advertising: You can typically limit ad tracking or opt out of personalized advertising through your mobile device's settings.
    • For Android devices: Navigate to Settings > Google > Ads (or Privacy > Ads) and adjust settings like "Opt out of Ads Personalization" or "Delete advertising ID."
    • For iOS devices: Navigate to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking (ensure "Allow Apps to Request to Track" is off for this app if prompted) or Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising and adjust "Personalized Ads."
    (The exact path may vary slightly depending on your device and OS version.)

    Opting out of personalized ads does not mean you will no longer see ads; it means the ads displayed may be less relevant to your interests.

  • Users in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, and Switzerland:

    Google AdMob is required to comply with Google's EU User Consent Policy. When you first use the Application, or as required by AdMob, you should be presented with a consent mechanism (often through a Consent Management Platform - CMP) regarding the collection and use of your data for personalized advertising. You have the right to manage and withdraw your consent through such mechanisms.

    You also have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent local laws, including:

    • The right to access your personal data.
    • The right to rectify inaccurate personal data.
    • The right to request erasure of your personal data.
    • The right to restrict the processing of your personal data.
    • The right to data portability.
    • The right to object to the processing of your personal data.

    To exercise these rights with respect to data collected by AdMob, you should typically contact Google directly as per their privacy policy. You can also contact us at laxeneo@gmail.com for guidance on how to reach Google or if you have general privacy questions about our Application.

7. Data Retention

  • EduLingua: As we do not directly collect or store your personally identifiable information, we do not have specific retention policies for it.
  • Google AdMob: Data collected by Google AdMob is subject to Google's own data retention policies. Please refer to Google's privacy policies for details.
  • Any aggregated or anonymized usage statistics that we might view through AdMob's dashboard (which do not identify you personally) are used by us to understand app usage trends and improve the Application.

8. Children's Privacy

This Application is not intended for or directed at children under the age of 13 (or a higher age threshold as required by applicable local laws, e.g., 16 in some parts of the EU for GDPR consent). We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with information without your consent, please contact us at laxeneo@gmail.com. If we become aware that information has been collected from a child without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information or request its removal by our partners.

9. Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is handled responsibly. While we do not directly collect or store your personal data, we rely on the security measures implemented by Google AdMob and your mobile device's operating system. Please understand that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page. Your continued use of the Application after any changes or revisions to this Privacy Policy shall indicate your agreement with the terms of such revised Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, or if you have any concerns about your privacy while using the Application, please do not hesitate to contact us at laxeneo@gmail.com
